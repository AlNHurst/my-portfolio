About Me

Hello! My name is Alexandra. I have bachelor’s degrees in chemistry and psychology and a master’s degree in the chemistry from the University of North Carolina Charlotte. I have also completed all the courses to satisfy the secondary education minor requirements (excluding student teaching). Currently I am a PhD student in Nanoscale Science at UNC Charlotte. I am a tutor in general and organic chemistry courses. After in-person instruction transitioned to online learning during the COVID-19 pandemic, the demand for online learning resources and tutors increased. My career goal is to develop technology-based learning resources for challenging math and science courses.
